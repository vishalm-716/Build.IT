EMBERLINK V2 RESCUE PLAN

1. Reframe the product honestly.
   - Not "internet still works".
   - It is a local-first DTN mesh node simulator.

2. Fix the packet lifecycle.
   - Messages now support TTL, deduplication, received/archive states, and feed cleanup.

3. Reduce clutter.
   - Live feed excludes archived traffic by default.
   - Operators can mark packets received or archive them.

4. Reduce misuse.
   - Relay, mission, capsule, and message-state changes require an operator key.
   - Civilian packet input is rate-limited.
   - Duplicate submissions inside a short window are suppressed.

5. Improve realism.
   - Different relays drain at different rates.
   - Charging is gradual, not instant.
   - Relays auto-sleep at zero battery.

6. Strengthen the story for judges.
   - Explain that aliens killed long-range centralized infrastructure, not all local compute.
   - Nearby devices and portable relays still exchange compact packets during contact windows.

7. MVP claim.
   - This is a browser-based console for one resilient node.
   - The next real-world version would be Android/desktop clients using BLE, Wi-Fi Direct, LoRa, or portable relay hardware.
